This document contains all information pertaining to the release of the first version of running prototype of algorithmic trading software labeled "BlueTitan". The intent is for this piece of software to maintain autonomous, repetitive trading strategies in order to leverage the inefficiencies of a market and generate profit. The current version's purpose is to demonstrate a running version of the bot and to provide a basis of data in which to optimize and improve its performance. The following task list comprises of the criteria that needs to be met in order for this bot to be considered 'running'. The target date is July 1st.

- Balancing that guarantees no loss
  - Automatic balancing
  - Fee accounting
- Simple profit liquidation cause
- Metrics, AssetMetrics cleanup
- Database functionality restored
